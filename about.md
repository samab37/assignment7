# <Strong> Who Am I?</strong>


Hi, I’m Sam, a lone human explorer traveling through the outer reaches of our solar system. I document what it feels like to stand on distant moons, drift through planetary rings, and witness worlds few have seen up close.

## What I Do

I explore, observe, and record. From Europa’s shifting ice to the glittering debris of Saturn’s rings, I share field notes, reflections, and quiet moments from life far beyond Earth.
